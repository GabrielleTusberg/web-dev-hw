About Me

I grew up in SW Portland Oregon.
I frequently miss my hometown but I also need a few years to explore elswhere. I moved to Missoula, Montana fairly randomly and have enjoyed it ever since.
I decided to attend the University of Montana after 6 months of living here and it was the best decision I ever made.
I sing and play guitar and like to write my own music so the audio file placed below is a recorded song I wrote and recorded with an old friend.
The song is called "Too Young," and I hope you will enjoy listening to it while baking your delicious cookies.
